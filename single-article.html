Symptoms of renal colic

  • 1- Sudden sharp pain in the lower back, reaching the other areas of the back (at the site of the kidneys)
  • 2- Pain during urination
  • 3- High temperature in cases of infection
  • 4- Tremor or shivering
  • 5-Nausea and vomiting, in rare cases
  • 6- Blood in the urine If it is not clear to the eye, we perform an analysis of the hidden blood test for the diagnosis of renal colic

Renal colic has been diagnosed using imaging methods such as: CT scan of the kidneys. The examination is carried out using a relatively small amount of radiation - and in some cases the doctor may recommend a urological examination with radiography with contrast or ultrasound.
